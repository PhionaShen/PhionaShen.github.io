On 18 April 2018, THE United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) officially appointed Mr. Wang Junkai as "UNEP Goodwill Ambassador". In his speech at the ceremony, he said he would study and work with a more humble attitude: "I also realize that environmental protection work is not a matter of a day, only persistent efforts can see results." Later, Kai Boss attended the launch ceremony of unep's "China Earth Defender Youth Award" and called on Chinese youth to pay attention to the broader field of environmental protection. On April 10, 2019, the Organizing committee of APEC Future China officially announced that Mr. Wang Junkai, a young actor and singer, will be the 2019 APEC Future Young Ambassador. As an outstanding young artist, Wang junkai pursues progress and is enthusiastic about public welfare and charity. On March 5, 2021, Mr. Wang Junkai, the publicity ambassador of public interest litigation and procuratorial work, visited the satellite Remote sensing application Laboratory, ecological environment testing laboratory and food testing laboratory of the Judicial Appraisal Center of the SPP.
